### Small guide for setup the home page

1. Since those images are generated by AI we couldn't make them perfect, so you may need to avoid this:
    - Ignore the navbar that's available on those generated images and use the same navbar that we have on product page.
    - Product images on Home page may look different from those we have on Product page. Please use those from our API.

2. Buy button should take you to the product page of each product exist on the home page. URL will be like this `yourDomainURL/product/productId`
